About Us

Chop Shop Salads is a locally owned, fast casual dining restaurant. By utilizing the highest quality ingredients, we ensure that our salads, soups and wraps provide our customers with the finest dining experience.

With over 20 chef-designed dressings, more than a dozen homemade soups, as well as our freshly grilled proteins, you will find a variety and quality that will keep you coming back.

Brad Vetter - Owner
